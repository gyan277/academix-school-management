# How to Change Academic Year - Quick Guide

## For School Admins (No Technical Knowledge Required)

### Step 1: Login
- Open your school management system
- Login with your admin credentials

### Step 2: Go to Settings
- Click on **Settings** in the sidebar
- Click on the **Profile** tab

### Step 3: Change Academic Year
- Find the **Academic Year** field
- It currently shows something like "2024/2025"
- Click in the field and change it to your desired year
- Example: "2025/2026" or "2026/2027"

### Step 4: Save
- Click the **Save School Settings** button
- You'll see a success message

### Step 5: Done!
- The new academic year is now active
- All pages (Academic, Attendance, Finance, etc.) will use the new year
- Your old data is still safe in the database

## What Happens When You Change the Year?

### ✅ What's Preserved
- All student grades from previous years
- All attendance records from previous years
- All payment records from previous years
- All teacher assignments from previous years

### 🆕 What Starts Fresh
- No grades for the new year (you'll enter them as the year progresses)
- No attendance records for the new year (you'll mark them daily)
- No teacher assignments for the new year (you'll need to assign teachers)
- No term definitions for the new year (you'll need to set them up)

## After Changing the Year

### Things You Need to Do:

1. **Assign Teachers to Classes**
   - Go to Settings → Teachers
   - Assign each teacher to their classes for the new year

2. **Set Up Terms**
   - Go to Settings → Terms
   - Define Term 1, Term 2, Term 3 with dates

3. **Configure Subjects**
   - Go to Settings → Subjects
   - Assign subjects to each class

4. **Set Fee Structure**
   - Go to Finance → Set Fees
   - Define fees for each class

5. **Promote Students** (if needed)
   - Go to Registrar → Classes
   - Use "Promote All" button to move students to next class
   - JHS3 students will be graduated

## Switching Back to View Old Data

You can always switch back to view previous years' data:

1. Go to Settings → Profile
2. Change academic year back to "2024/2025"
3. Click Save
4. Now you can view all the old grades, attendance, etc.
5. Switch back to current year when done

## Tips

- **Best Time to Change**: At the start of a new school year
- **Backup First**: Consider exporting data before changing (Settings → Export Data)
- **Plan Ahead**: Set up the new year's structure before students arrive
- **Test First**: Try changing and switching back to make sure you're comfortable

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issues:
1. Check that you clicked "Save School Settings"
2. Refresh your browser page
3. Contact your system administrator
4. Check the browser console for error messages (F12 key)

The academic year system is designed to be simple and safe. Your data is always preserved, and you can switch between years anytime to view historical records.
